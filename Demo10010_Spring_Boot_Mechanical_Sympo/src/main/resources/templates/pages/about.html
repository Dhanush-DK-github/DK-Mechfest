Our College - ACGCET

Established in 1952 by a leading educationist of the time, RM. Alagappa Chettiar, ACCET is today truly an asset for engineering education in the State just for its results and campus infrastructure. Read More..

Our Department - Mechanical

Department of Mechanical Engineering was introduced in the year 1952 with the student intake of 40. The sanctioned intake for B.E.-Mechanical Engineering has been increased to 60 in the year 1989 and 120 in the year 2009. Read More..

Mechasso - ACGCET Mechanical Association

MECHASSO is also called as Mechanical Association which indulges itself in improving our Mechanical Department. It also conducts two symposium FUEL & SHACKLES.

Mechfest - ACGCET Mechanical Festival

MECHFEST is also called as Mechanical Festival conducted by ACGCET MECHASSO, where both the Inter (SHACKLES) and Intra (FUEL) Level Symposium conducted on consecutive days, which is also followed by Workshops.

Mechfest will be celebrated on Oct 11, 12, 13 - 2023.
